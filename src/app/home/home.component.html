"The Great Gatsby" by F. Scott Fitzgerald"

Set in the 1920s, this classic novel explores themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream through the captivating story of Jay Gatsby.

"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee:

This Pulitzer Prize-winning novel delves into racial injustice and morality as seen through the eyes of young Scout Finch in the fictional town of Maycomb, Alabama.

"Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen

A beloved romance novel, it follows the spirited Elizabeth Bennet as she navigates societal expectations, family dynamics, and her complex relationship with the enigmatic Mr. Darcy.
